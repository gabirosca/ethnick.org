Friends

Friends of ETHNICK are individuals and organizations that have pledged their support to ETHNICK, and the programs and activates associated. Support includes but is not limited to volunteering time, distributing leadership information, and monetary donations. Invitations to become a Friend of ETHNICK are extended by the Board of Directors, however if you are interested in our work and wish to support it please do not hesitate to contact us.

By becoming a Friend of ETHNICK, individuals and organizations will receive a monthly update on program activities and may be consulted when the organization makes significant changes in its activities or is looking at developing new programs. Friends will also receive tickets to the yearly graduation ceremony and the annual ETHNICK, Inc. Awards Dinner /Gala.
