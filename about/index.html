Mission

ETHNICK establishes international exchange opportunities that address issues of mutual interest to the United States and other countries and should benefit a population that is not being addressed through existing authorized exchanges. ETHNICK seeks to increase mutual understanding between the people of the United States and the people of other countries by means of educational and cultural exchange programs, including the exchange of scholars, researchers, professionals, students, and educators. Educational and cultural engagement is premised on the knowledge that mutual understanding, the development of future leaders, and the benefits of education programs influence societies and affect official decision-making almost everywhere in the world today.

Goals

  • Position ETHNICK, Inc. as a credible and authoritative source for professional development and cultural awareness.
  • Increase students and young professionals opportunities to better themselves professional and personally.
  • Increase students and young professional’s competiveness in the ever evolving job market.
  • Develop an active network of young leaders who support each other’s goals while also supporting the development of the next generation of leaders.

Values & Guiding Principles

  • Develop Leaders
  • Increase Cultural Awareness Domestically and Abroad
  • Empowering Youth and Young Professionals
  • Support International Development
