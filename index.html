ETHNICK

“ETHNICK” is a nonprofit organization in the United States of America, established to develop future world leaders. Through cultural exchange and first hand leadership experiences, exposure to various cultures, systems of government, ways of thinking and professional opportunities ETHNICK’s programs provide a unique opportunity for all participants. ETHNICK offers various enrichment programs for students and young professionals from the United States and abroad.

ETHNICK’s core mission is to develop better leaders through networking in cultural knowledge. The concept of education through networking is twofold. First, is learning about one’s personal network, family history and personal contacts, secondly, understanding the importance of the network of people that an individual surrounds them with. Cultural knowledge consists of the understanding differences and similarities between cultures to better understand the successes and failures of other cultures.
